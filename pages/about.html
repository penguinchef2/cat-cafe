OUR STORY

A. Thomas, B. Renner, A. Morgan, A. Ajithkumar

Owners

Our café was founded on the love of cats with the desire to build a cozy, welcoming space for both people and animals.

We partner with local shelters to provide a safe, comfortable environment where cats can relax and meet potential owners.

Every cat here has their own personality and story, and we’re proud to help them find their forever homes!

From humble beginnings, our café has grown into a favorite local spot known for its cozy atmosphere and friendly, adoptable cats.
