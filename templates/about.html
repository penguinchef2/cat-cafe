About Cat Cafe

A cozy place where cats and people find each other.

OUR STORY

Who We Are

A. Thomas, B. Renner, A. Morgan, and A. Ajithkumar

Our café was created to be more than just a place to grab a drink — it's a warm, welcoming space where people can relax, connect, and meet adoptable cats.

Our Mission

We partner with local shelters to give cats a safe and comfortable environment while they wait for their forever homes.

Every cat here has a unique personality, and we strive to match them with the right people through meaningful interactions.

Why It Matters

By combining a café experience with adoption opportunities, we help reduce shelter overcrowding while creating a relaxing space for our community.
